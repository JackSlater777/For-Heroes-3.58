HoA Necromancy

Necromancy: the skill percentages are decreased to 5% at basic level (instead of 10), 10% at advanced level (instead of 20) and 15% at expert level (instead of 30).
Amulet of the Undertaker: artifact increases Necromancy skill +2.5 % (instead of 5).
Vampire's Cowl: artifact increases Necromancy skill +5% (instead of 10).
Dead Man's Boots: artifact increases Necromancy skill +7.5% (instead of 15).
Necromancy Amplifier: structure increases Necromancy skill +5% (instead of 10).
